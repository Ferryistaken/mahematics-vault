Function analysis is the study of vector spaces and [[Linear Functions]].

Function Analysis at our level includes:
1. [[Domain]]
2. [[Sign]]
3. [[Intersections]]
4. [[Limits]]
	- [[Asymptotes]]
		- [[Vertical Asymptotes]]
		- [[Horizontal Asymptotes]]
		- [[Oblique Asymptotes]]
	- [[Boundedness]]
	- [[Singularities]]
	- [[Continuity]]
